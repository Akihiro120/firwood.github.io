Project Atmosphere

Download

In the year 2067, within an advanced technological civilisation, a government-approved project was started - Project: Atmosphere. Project Atmosphere was started to fully harness the energy composition of the Earth, but a fault was made in calculating the elemental structure causing an unstable source of matter to be created Termporial Matter which had the effect on everything in contact, creating major instability in the Earth's processes, causing shifts in temperature, climate, electrical power, and creating significant mutations to all biological matter, all human life forms were transformed into an unknown type of human species due to our unique genetic makeup. As a result, you and some others were protected from this compound and remained normal with no repercussions. Once you wake up you will be tasked with recovering what was once built by a civilisation
